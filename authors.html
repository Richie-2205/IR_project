Information for Authors

Guidelines, requirements, and resources for submission

    Guidelines for Manuscript Writing and Submission

  • Every manuscript should conform to a high standard of original research article which have not been published elsewhere or under consideration by anybody either in part or whole in any language.
  • All manuscripts should be written in English Language and must not be more than 6,000 words (17 pages) including references, tables, figures and graphs.
